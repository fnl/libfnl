===============================
kappa -- Inter-Rater Agreements
===============================

.. automodule:: libfnl.stat.kappa

This module holds various functions to calculate the agreements of votes or
annotations made by raters or annotators on a set of subjects. The votes or
annotations are all treated as categorical values.
